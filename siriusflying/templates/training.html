Area


Sirius Flying is conveniently located near complex SFO airspace. We are also within reasonable distance to as for north to Napa, far south to Monterey, and as for east to the Central Valley. And on occasion, just on the outer scope of grasp is Lake Tahoe.

Certificates and Ratings


  • Private - The private certificate is one of your first gate ways or “drivers licenses” to the sky. You will learn the fundamentals of helicopters, aviation, safety, confidence, and pilot competency.
  • Instrument - Learn to fly the helicopter by the instruments. You will be taught about the complex system in the sky that helps guide IFR air traffic. You will learn to fly in the event of adverse weather. *Flying to get your instrument rating is an effective way to build hours necessary for a commercial rating later on in your carrier.
  • Commercial - Learn the art to commercial flying and earn the privilege to be paid to fly! Learn from seasoned CFIs, who have actual experience in the commercial world.
  • Initial Certified Flight Instructor - Get the tools necessary in order to now teach people how to fly helicopters! Teach others your passion for flying. Master man and machine. Attaining your certified flight instructor certificate is a great way to be able to build hours and get paid for it!
  • Certified Flight Instructor (Instrument) - Add the ability to teach pilots to fly in adverse weather. Increase your knowledge about weather and it's hazards. Getting your CFI-Instrument rating on top of your current ratings makes you sought after in the commercial world.
  • ATP - Brush up on anything you may not have done in a while and prepare for the checkride.

Equipment


Sirius Flying utilizes safe, reliable, and proven aircraft for flight instruction. We fly the Robinson R-44 Raven II. The R-44 is a very capable machine that offers advantages over other aircraft. They are well maintained and cleaned regularly. Some perks about our fleet are that each aircraft is equipped with air-conditioning, to increase your comfort level.

*In the near future, we will be adding a Robinson Cadet. It provides both the affordability of the R-22 and the safety of the R-44.

Fleet Highlights:

  • Stability Augmentation System (SAS) (simple auto-pilot)
  • Garmin G500 Instrument “Glass Panel” Display
  • Air-conditioning
  • GPS
