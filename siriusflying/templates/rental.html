Helicopter Rental


Sirius Flying Students and Graduates have the special opportunity to rent our helicopters.

AND

We also extend our rental privileges to NON-Sirius Flying affiliated persons with conditions

Requirements


In order to rent one of our aircraft without a Sirius Flying representative, some of the following will apply.

  • FAA Certificate and valid Currency
  • Valid medical and Biennial Flight Review (R44 Specific)
  • Pass a Sirius Flying checkout in ground and flight
  • Renters Insurance
  • Local Area only flights
  • By appointment

*Alternatively, if you do not meet one of the preceding, you can still rent an aircraft with the provision that a CFI recommended by Sirius Flying be in the front seat with the renter.
