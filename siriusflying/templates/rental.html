Helicopter Rental


As a student or graduate of Sirius Flying training, our helicopters are available for you to rent. You will need to meet applicable FAA and Sirius Flying standards for the particular type of rental. For example, a local solo flight will have different requirements from a full-passenger flight to Tahoe.

We are committed to your continued proficiency and pilot safety, so you can spend more time enjoying time in the air; alone or with your passengers.

AVAILABILITY


Helicopter Rental is available Friday through Tuesday and is not available on Wednesday or Thursday.

Rental hours are as follows:

Time Hours
Pacific Standard Time (PST) 7:00AM - 4:00PM
Pacific Daylight Time (PDT) 10:00AM - 7:00PM
