Discovery Flight


Our discovery flight consists of an affordable and fun introduction to helicopters and helicopter flight training. This is an excellent adventure for yourself or as a gift for someone wanting to experience the joy of flying helicopters.

You will spend 30 minutes with an instructor before the flight to learn about helicopter characteristics and flight controls. Then, we'll spend 45 minutes in the air, where you will be at the controls during most of the flight. If you are feeling adventurous that day, we'll also introduce you to helicopter autorotations performed by the instructor, or take you to the foothills for off airport operations.

After the flight, we'll spend another 30 minutes to answer any question you may have about the flight or the program. There is no obligation for you to continue lessons, but if you wish to pursue a rating, the actual flying time accumulated during the discovery flight is applicable as an introductory lesson toward the rating you pursue and you will receive a logbook in which the flight is recorded.
