Discovery Flight


Are you interested in flying a helicopter? Want to try and fly a helicopter? Get HANDS ON experiences at the controls!

  • You will get the chance to manipulate the controls of a helicopter in flight, get a demonstration of the uniqueness of helicopters, and the perspective of a life time.
  • Bring an observer. Bring a friend, a co-worker, or a spouse.

Flight Details


The Discovery Flight is 45 minutes of flight instruction preceded by a half-hour safety briefing.

During the flight portion, you will control the heading and speed of the helicopter as it crosses the bay en-route to a beautiful area north of Calaveras Reservoir. There, I will demonstrate off-airport operations and auto-rotation (the helicopter glide).

Returning to Palo Alto, we will spend the rest of the time learning how to hover the helicopter 5 feet above the ground. The flight is actual training which can be credited towards a pilot's certificate. We will issue a starter log-book with an official record of the flight.
