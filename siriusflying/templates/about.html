Rick Gregory, Owner


Rick is an experienced flight instructor and a successful Intellectual Property Attorney. He founded Sirius Flying LLC with a fleet including a Robinson R22 Beta II helicopter, a Husky airplane annd a Boeing Stearman biplane. Rick has logged over 10,000 hours of flying experience. His mission is to provide an excellent aviation experience to discerning people. rick@siriusflying.com


John Pyle, Chief Pilot


John began his flying career in 1967 flying helicopters as an officer in the US Army. After training at Ft. Wolters, Texas and Ft. Rucker, Alabama, he commanded gunship units with the First Calvary Division in combat in Viet Nam 1967-1968.From 1968 to 1970, he was stationed at Ft. Bragg, North Carolina, where he commanded Headquarters Company, Simmons Army Airfield Command.

John joined the Chicago Fire Department from 1970-1972 then flew helicopters in New Zealand in pilot training, agricultural work, venison recovery and commercial lifting. In 1991, he began instructing in California in helicopters and airplanes.

Since 1994, John has been an FAA Designated Pilot Examiner which allows him to give flight tests and issue pilot licenses. He has logged over 18,000 hours of flying.

There is no greater pleasure for John Pyle than to be in the sky sharing the joy and love of safe flying. john@siriusflying.com
