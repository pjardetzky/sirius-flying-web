John Pyle, Owner


john@siriusflying.com

John has 18,000 hours of pilot experience. Most of those hours were flying helicopters. His helicopter pilot experience includes:

  • Armed combat in Vietnam
  • Venison hunting and recovery in New Zealand’s Southern Alps
  • Commercial operations such as Agricultural application, external lifting, surveying, etc.
  • Private fun flying

But the bulk of his experience has been in training people like you to become helicopter pilots. John is also a FAA Designated Pilot Examiner. This allows him to administer helicopter pilot flight tests and issue pilot certificates and ratings.

John currently charges $95 per hour for ground and flight instruction.

Simon Watsky, CFI


simon@siriusflying.com

Simon has been flying helicopters since 2008, and instructing since 2010. Between 2009 and 2012 he completed a three-year apprenticeship as an Airframe and Powerplant Mechanic, and in December 2012 he received his A&P certificate after a 16 hour examination. Although he prefers flying, he views an understanding of the mechanical operation of aircraft to be as important as “cyclic and collective” skills. From 2011-2012 he completed transition training, adding on airplane ratings to his helicopter certificates. He has over 1,000 flight hours the vast majority of which are in helicopters.

Simon currently charges $85 per hour for ground and flight instruction. This rate is discounted to $75/hr when purchasing blocks of 10 hours.
