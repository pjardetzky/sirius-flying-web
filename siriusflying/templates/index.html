Sirius Flying, LLC


Sirius Flying, LLC is committed to providing difinitive helicopter flight training and rental services to recreational and professional pilots. Our instructors are all high-time certified flight instructors who consistently surpass FAA standards and our chief pilot is the FAA designated pilot examiner (DPE) for the San Jose area.

Come fly with us!


If you are looking to learn how to fly helicopters, look no further. We offer a range of flight training options taylored to your individual goals, experience and ability. Whether you are just starting in aviation or adding a type or instrument rating, Sirius Flying LLC will train you to the highest level of competence and safety whilst you experience the joy of piloting helicopters.

Our instructors provide comprehensive FAA Part 61 helicopter flight training for private pilot and instrument pilot ratings.

Explore The Bay Area By Air


We are located in Palo Alto, the center of Silicon Valley, with convenient access north toward San Francisco or south toward Monterey. The Bay Area also offers several 4000 foot mountains, exquisite coastline and secluded landscapes for off-airport training and adventures.
