Overview


Sirius Flying offers flight instruction for those who seek it, helicopter rentals for those who are rated, and professional helicopter services for those in need. Regardless of what you are looking for, Sirius Flying can accommodate your request. Please give us a call and we can see how best to meet your needs.

Our Mission Statement: “We strive to bring our clients a higher level of helicopter service with safety, efficiency, and punctuality.”

Our locational scope is the major San Francisco Bay to the outlying areas.

Discovery Flight


Are you interested in FLYING A HELICOPTER? Want to try and FLY A HELICOPTER? Get HANDS ON experiences at the controls! You will get the chance to manipulate the controls of a helicopter in flight, get a demonstration of the uniqueness of helicopters, and the perspective of a life time. You can also bring along an observer, a friend, a sibling, or a spouse.

Call today to schedule your flight!

Flight Details

  • 15 minutes of ground explaining helicopter controls
  • Receive an endorsement to manipulate the controls
  • Answering any additional questions you may have
  • 45 minutes of flight where you will control the heading and speed of the helicopter as it crosses the bay to a beautiful area north of Calaveras Reservoir.
  • Be demonstrated off-airport operations and auto-rotation (the helicopter glide).
  • Hover the helicopter1.

The flight is actual training which can be credited towards a pilot's certificate. We will issue a starter log-book with an official record of the flight. Upon request, you can be scheduled in our aircraft that has “Stability Augmentation System” (SAS), which can help with an easier flying experience.

1 Hovering the helicopter is subject to space available on the ramp at the time of the flight.
