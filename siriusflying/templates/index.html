Sirius Flying


Sirius Flying provides helicopter flight training and rental services to people who wish to fly for their own pleasure or business. Our instructors are experienced professional pilots. The Chief Pilot, John Pyle, is also an FAA Designated Pilot Examiner (DPE).

Come fly with us!


If you are looking to learn how to fly helicopters, look no further. We offer a range of flight training options tailored to your individual goals, experience and ability. Whether you are just starting in aviation or adding a type or instrument rating, Sirius Flying will train you to the highest level of competence and safety whilst you experience the joy of piloting helicopters.

Our instructors provide comprehensive FAA Part 61 helicopter flight training for private pilot and instrument pilot ratings.

Explore The Bay Area By Air


We are located at Palo Alto Airport, in California's San Francisco Bay Area. The Bay Areas offers beautiful mountains, valleys, coast-line and multiple airports that facilitate comprehensive and exciting pilot training. It is amazing and fun to fly here.
